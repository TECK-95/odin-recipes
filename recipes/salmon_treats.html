Salmon Treats

Description:

While most dogs and their owners will often go for chicken or red meat, sometimes fish is a great alternative. Salmon is one of the popular choices in this case, and your dog will be sure to love digging in to this simple to make treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 14.75 ounce can salmon
  • 1 cup brown rice flour
  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup diced fresh parsley

Note: Ingredients are for roughly 30 treats. Will need to be adjusted depending on batch size.
