Chicken Treats

Description:

Chicken is a popular ingredient in any dog food, so of course it would make for a popular dog treat ingredient. If your dog loves chicken, this is a great simple treat to whip up for them. Make sure to let Fido watch and enjoy the baking process!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 container nonfat yogurt (5.3 ounces)
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 egg

Note: Ingredients are for roughly 45 treats. Will need to be adjusted depending on batch size.
