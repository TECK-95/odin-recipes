Chicken Treats

Description:

Chicken is a popular ingredient in any dog food, so of course it would make for a popular dog treat ingredient. If your dog loves chicken, this is a great simple treat to whip up for them. Make sure to let Fido watch and enjoy the baking process!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 container nonfat yogurt (5.3 ounces)
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 egg

Note: Ingredients are for roughly 45 treats. Will need to be adjusted depending on batch size.

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In medium bowl, combine ingredients and stir thoroughly (batter will be thick and sticky)
  3. Using a tablespoon, drop batter onto baking sheets lined with parchment and flatten slightly with spoon (biscuits will spread very little so they should be close together)
  4. Bake until lightly golden brown (usually 12-14 minutes)
  5. Cool completely on wire racks and store in refrigerator or freezer for longer (thaw before feeding)
