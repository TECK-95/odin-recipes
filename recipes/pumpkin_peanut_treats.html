Pumpkin and Peanut Butter Treats

Description:

Much like people, dogs sometimes get a bit of a sweet tooth. These pumpkin and peanut butter treats are the perfect thing in that case. Your dog is almost sure to love the taste and texture of peanut butter, and a bit of pumpkin on top of that will bring out the flavour all the more. Pumpkin also helps settle the stomach, so there's that added benefit as well!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon water, or more, if necessary for batter texture

Note: Ingredients are for roughly 25 treats. Will need to be adjusted depending on batch size.
