Pumpkin and Peanut Butter Treats

Description:

Much like people, dogs sometimes get a bit of a sweet tooth. These pumpkin and peanut butter treats are the perfect thing in that case. Your dog is almost sure to love the taste and texture of peanut butter, and a bit of pumpkin on top of that will bring out the flavour all the more. Pumpkin also helps settle the stomach, so there's that added benefit as well!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon water, or more, if necessary for batter texture

Note: Ingredients are for roughly 25 treats. Will need to be adjusted depending on batch size.

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine ingredients in a bowl and stir with spatula (may be easier to begin with dry ingredients)
  3. Once ingredients are combined, transfer mix to work surface and use hands to further work it together (add water 1 teaspoon at a time if necessary but keep mix dry and stiff)
  4. Using a roller, roll the dough until it is roughly 1/2 inch thick
  5. Cut into desired pieces (usually 1/2 inch) and place onto parchment-lined baking sheet
  6. Bake until golden brown and crunchy (usually about 40-50 minutes)
  7. Cool before serving and store in refrigerator or freezer (thaw before serving or even warm them up!)
